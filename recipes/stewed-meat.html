Beef Stew Recipe

Beef stew is a universal food

Ingridients

  • 1 tbsp. (or more) vegetable oil
  • 2 lb. beef chuck stew meat, cut into 1" cubes
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled, cut into rounds
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/4 c. tomato paste
  • 6 c. low-sodium beef broth
  • 1 c. red wine
  • 1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 fresh thyme sprigs
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 lb. baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 c. frozen peas
  • 1/4 c. chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. In a large Dutch oven or heavy pot over medium heat, heat oil.
  2. Add beef and cook
  3. Cook onion, carrots, and celery, stirring, until softened
  4. Add garlic and tomato paste and cook
  5. Add potatoes and simmer, covered, until potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes.
  6. Remove bay leaves and thyme.
  7. Divide stew among bowls. Top with parsley.
