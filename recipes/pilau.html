Pilau

How to make Pilau

Pilau is an Kenyan/Indian food either with meat or No-meat

Ingridients

  • 3 Cups rice
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 medium size tomatoes
  • 2 tbsps finely grated fresh garlic
  • 2 tbsps finely grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tsp finely grated fresh turmeric
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 & ½ tsps pilau masala spice
  • 1 & ½ tsps salt
  • 10 tbsps cooking oil to fry
  • 6 cups hot water

Steps

  1. In a suitable clean pan you’re using to cook the pilau, add the salad oil and place it on the fire.
  2. Add the onion and fry to a golden color. Stir frequently.
  3. Add the garlic, ginger, and fresh turmeric.
  4. Add the tomato at this point.
  5. Stir in the tomato paste, 2 tablespoons of dhania, and the green pepper.
  6. Next, stir in the rice and continue stirring until all the ingredients are evenly blended together.
  7. Use a fork to gently turn the rice, allowing the ingredients to mix into the rice evenly.
  8. Cover again and let the rice cook for another 10 or so minutes.
  9. Gently turn your rice again with a fork then cover.
